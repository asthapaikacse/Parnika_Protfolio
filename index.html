01. About Me

My journey in technology began at Chitkara University, where I laid my foundations in Computer Science during my B.Tech. Driven by a passion for technical depth and academic excellence, I transitioned to Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology (TIET) for my M.Tech in CSE.

Currently, my research is centered around Medical Imaging and Deep Learning. I've developed solutions in Pneumonia Detection, deriving state-of-the-art accuracy on benchmark datasets. My focus extends to the intersection of healthcare and AI.

I am deeply interested in Self-Supervised Learning and its potential to unlock insights from vast amounts of unlabeled data, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in modern AI systems.

Projects

Fake News Classification

📋
Research Project

Deep learning-based fake news detection using ISOT and LIAR datasets. Implemented and compared RNN, LSTM, BiLSTM, GRU, ALBERT, RoBERTa, and DeBERTa. Built a hybrid BiLSTM + DeBERTa + ALBERT ensemble with masked mean pooling for improved generalization.

PyTorch HuggingFace BERT DeBERTa RoBERTa ALBERT BiLSTM Transformers NLP Streamlit Python

Brain Stroke Detection

🧠
Research Project

Vision Transformer (ViT) model for brain stroke classification from CT scans achieving 95.2% accuracy vs 81% CNN baseline. Fine-tuned ViT-Base (16×16 patches, 12 layers, 12 attention heads) with AdamW optimizer and data augmentation.

Vision Transformer ViT-Base Medical Imaging Deep Learning AdamW Data Augmentation Streamlit Python

Parkinson's Disease Detection

🔬
Research Project

Predictive system using clinical data of 2,105 patients with 35 features. Compared CatBoost, XGBoost, LightGBM, Random Forest, SVM, and MLP — achieving 96.67% accuracy with CatBoost. Applied SMOTE for class imbalance and feature engineering.

CatBoost XGBoost LightGBM Random Forest SVM SMOTE Scikit-Learn Streamlit Python

Autonomous Obstacle Avoidance Car

🚗

Autonomous vehicle using Arduino Nano and ultrasonic sensors for real-time distance measurement. Servo-based steering control dynamically changes direction when obstacles are detected within a 15 cm range.

Arduino Nano Ultrasonic Sensor Servo Motor L298N Embedded C++ IoT OpenCV
